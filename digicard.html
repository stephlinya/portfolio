Overview.

Problem

Designing your own business card can be stressful. There are so many different styles out there, and outsource printing can be pricey.

Solution

A personalized digital business card. A digital business card can be an interactive and cost-effective way to share your contact information. Digital business cards are sustainable and convenient. Take it with you whereever you go.

Role

I worked on this project independently and completed the design and code myself.

Project status

This project is in progress. Future versions will allow users to adjust information details and colorways, offer downloadable files, and sample library.
