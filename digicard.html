Overview.

Problem

Designing your own business card can be stressful. There are so many different styles out there, and outsource printing can be pricey.

Solution

A personalized digital business card. A digital business card can be an interactive and cost-effective way to share your contact information. Digital business cards are sustainable and convenient. Take it with you wherever you go.

The website

A responsive form that receives user input and generates a personalized business card.

Role

I worked on this project independently and completed the design and code.

Project status

This project is in progress. Future versions will allow users to adjust information details and colorways and offer downloadable link to business card.
