Overview.

Problem

Affirmations are difficult to think of and hard to accept. Placing affirmations around you can be helpful, but also can be easy to ignore.

Solution

An interactive chrome extension that encourages self-compassion and mindfulness through affirmations.

Role

I worked on this project independently and completed the design, code, and quotes myself.

Project status

This project is completed and available for download on the chrome extension webstore. I do hope to continue offering version updates with more affirmations and improved transitions.
